Hello, from Ten Commandments

We are a guild of fair players in Nefera with the goal of teaming up to kill bosses, complete quests, level up, and most importantly, have fun! If you are interested in joining don't hesitate to apply at our guild page, or contact one of the leaders.

Events

Every once in a while we will be hosting events both private events for guild members only and public events where anyone can participate. Rewards can range from semi-rare up to a hefty sum of Tibia Coins. Events will be posted onto our website, our discord, and the Nefera boards if it is a public event. For upcoming events you can check our calendar page.

Quests

We run quest services for Pits of Inferno and Inquisition fairly often with services for Wrath of the Emperor and In Service of Yalahar based on demand. Quest Services are free for guild members and allies but we simply ask you to come prepared and leave the loot for those who are clearing the areas.quests page.
